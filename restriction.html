Please Wear A Mask And Socially Distance To Help Protect Yourself and Others

These are the restrictions that have been placed in order to help slow the curve of COVID-19 Cases in Ontario




Gatherings:

  • Private Gatherings (home, backyard, parks):
    • Indoors: 10 People
    • Outdoors: 25 People
  • Organized Public Events (Event Spaces)
    • Indoors: 50 People
    • Outdoors: 100 People
  • Religious, Weddings, and Funeral Services
    • Indoors: 30% Capacity
    • Outdoors: 100 People

Rules for Businesses

  • Screen Employees
  • Limit Capacity So Guests Can Keep 2 Metres Apart
  • Masks Are Mandatory
  • Make Sure Workers Wear PPE
  • Clean And Disinfect Common Touched Surfaces
  • Manage Lineups To Ensure Social Distancing
  • Create A Safety Plan

Food and Drink Establishments (restaurants, bars)

  • Guests Must:
    • Sit With Atleast 2 Metres Between Tables
    • Wear Masks At All Times, Except When Eating
    • Give Accurate Contact Information

Recreational and Sport Facilities Capacity Limits

    • 50 Spectators
  • Outdoors
