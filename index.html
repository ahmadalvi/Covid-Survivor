Who We Are

The COVID-19 pandemic has not been easy to anyone. Millions have lost their lives,
people have been left jobless. Thats why Covid Survivor was created! Covid Survivor
is your one-stop website for the latest news and updates of COVID-19 in Ontario. This
This website will provide you with all the important news of COVID-19 Ontario, including
the latest case counts, hotspots, restrictions, tips on how to tackle COVID-19,
tricks to minimise your risk of COVID-19, blog posts from people who have defeated COVID-19,
the ability to submit an article related to COVID-19 that will benefit the general public.
Whats amazing is that you will also be able to directly communicate with previous covid-19 surviors
through our chat system!
